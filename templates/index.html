Services

Photos & Videos

Senior photographers provide you with the most fantastic wedding photography experience, record every moment of your wedding. Our photographers have average photography work experience for more than 10 years. Make sure that each photo is clear and beautiful, the photographer meticulously modifies it, then makes videos, making your wedding a precious memory forever.

Venue decoration

Inspired designers will create the most fancy wedding for you. Whether it is a romantic and relaxing seaside wedding or an exotic wedding, we will present you in the most perfect way. From the decoration of the small petal to the lighting of the atmosphere, we will strive for excellence and bring you the best wedding experience，also let every guest feel the incredible beauty of the wedding.

Process planning

A professional wedding planner will carefully plan every process of your wedding to ensure that every guest enjoys them and feels happy together. Solemn oaths and relaxed on-site interactions, each step naturally smooth transition. Explain the wedding process in detail to you, make detailed adjustments according to your needs and create a really fancy wedding for you.

Look Design

Senior wedding designers select the most suitable wedding dress for you and create the perfect makeup for you to better fit with the wedding theme. At the same time, professional hair stylist and makeup artist design a complete look for you. Magnify your beauty and you will be the most beautiful person of the wedding.

Live band

Different from other wedding companies, all background music will be played by professional bands. We aim to provide you with the most clear and shocking music to mobilize the wedding atmosphere. The live music of the band can be personally selected and you can confirm the performance before the wedding. At the same time, you can also dance with live band music in the after party.

Fashion

Relax, we have the perfect solution for you we will arrive at your home or hotel with plenty of time to remind you to put your garter on, figure out the clasp on your necklace and lace your corset up in that special, comfortable way as in your fitting.
